Px.Info. tab

In the Px.Info. tab you can see the information about a pixel. This is:
  • Pixel: The position and edge type (horizontal or vertical) of the selected pixel.
  • Mod.: The jump intensity between both sides of the edge pixel.
  • Rad.: The radius of the edge.
  • Slo.: The slope of the edge.
  • Dis.: The displacement respect to the center of the pixel.

If you want to see the information of a pixel, only you must make click with the middle button of the mouse in the image and then press the information button placed on the top of this tab. If the selected pixel isn't an edge pixel, the position and the text NO EDGE is shown.
